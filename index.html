The rundown

Din Tai Fung (鼎泰豐) started in Taipei and the original locations here are the gold standard. The B1 branch inside Taipei 101 is the most convenient after the 101 visit, or the Xinyi Anhe branch is nearby and less chaotic.

Order: xiao long bao (pork soup dumplings — minimum 2 steamers), shrimp and pork shao mai, shrimp fried rice, cucumber in garlic, red bean dumplings for dessert. For a group of 4, budget NT$800–1,200/person. Pre-order on Klook to reduce wait time.
